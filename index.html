ENIAC
ENCODING NETWORKING INNOVATING ACHIEVING COMPUTING

The official computer club of Bluebells School International is an initiative
managed completely by us, the students. ENIAC officially means Electronic
Numerical Integrator and Calculator, but to us it is much more than that. It means
Encoding, Networking, Innovating, Achieving and Computing. Overall it is a high
octane filled, adrenaline rush, powered by the brains of the members of the club.
Our previous events, VIRUS 2001, 2003 and Enter-o-Virus 2005, 2006, 2007,
2008, 2009, 2015 and 2016 were a roaring success, thanks to all the participating teams.
